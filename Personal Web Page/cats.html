Introducing Mango & Jingo

Why random cat page? Because they are cute.

Mango is the black & white tuxedo cat and Jingo is the white cat with brown-gray patches. They are very playful and cuddly 5 year old brothers. They have been taken to the shelter when they were kittens and were adopted by an acquaintance of mine. Due to circumstances, they were at the verge of going back to shelter when they were about 2 years old. I was planning to foster them a few months until they find good owner for them. However there was low chance for them to get adopted together since they have been together their whole lives. It has hard to come by someone who was willing to take 2 adult cats, and meanwhile they got super attached to me. I finally decided to adopt them and since then I have been serving my masters aka forever roommates.
