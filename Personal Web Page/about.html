About Me

Hi there, My name is Pamela.

This is my first website I'm creating in my CS journey. I know this may not look fancy and professional, but thanks for visiting and reading through. If you have suggestions how to make this better, don't hesitate to send me email on the contact page.

I'm located in sunny San Diego, California, with miles of beaches and lots of taco shops and breweries around. I earned my first bachelor's degree in University of California, San Diego as chemistry major. I always had an interest in programming, taking a few IT courses during my college years. In 2018, I finally decided to commit myself to learning computer science seriously.
